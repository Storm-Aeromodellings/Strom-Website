About US

Connecting People Through the Power of UAV's

Hi, I’m Shashikant Ramteke – UAV Enthusiast & Drone Testing Expert

Electronics and Telecommunication Engineer with practical expertise in Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) technologies. Proficient in operating a diverse range of UAV platforms, including fixed-wing, VTOL, and multi-rotor systems, in both manual and autonomous flight modes. Possesses extensive experience in aircraft piloting and thorough pre-deployment testing to validate operational integrity for diverse uses, including delivery, medical support, agriculture, mapping, surveillance, and inspections. Dedicated to practical innovation and the strategic application of technology.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to educate, inspire, and connect people through the world of UAV technology. We aim to make drone-related knowledge accessible and practical for everyone—from curious beginners to industry experts. By sharing insights, real-world applications, and updates, we strive to build a resourceful space where innovation and learning go hand in hand.

We envision a future where UAVs play a vital role in solving real-world problems across industries like defense, agriculture, logistics, and disaster management. Our goal is to be a leading platform that empowers individuals and organizations to explore, understand, and contribute to the ever-evolving drone ecosystem.

My Goal

Through this platform, I aim to share my knowledge and field experience with a wider audience—whether you're a beginner drone pilot or a company exploring UAV integration. This is not just a blog; it's a resource built on real-world flying and testing.

Multi Rotor UAV

A drone with multiple rotors (like 4 or more) that can take off, hover, and land vertically. Commonly used for short-range tasks like photography or surveillance.

Learn more

Fixed Wing UAV

A drone shaped like an airplane that flies using fixed wings. It’s good for long-distance flights but needs space to take off and land.

Learn more

VTOL Hybrid UAV

A drone that can take off and land vertically like a helicopter but fly like an airplane. Combines the benefits of both multi rotor and fixed wing types.
